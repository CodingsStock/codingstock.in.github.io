Social Media Optimization (SMO)

Social Media is one of the most important online communication tools to connect and share your personal/business ideas for better promotions and interactions with online community. This is a successful marketing avenue that should not be avoided and its ignorance can be a great loss of missing the broad spectrum business opportunity for any company or individual. Social media started with fun to interact with friends and families and turned into the professional & marketing aspects, which have become the basic necessity now and you can see that millions of users are increasing day by day in Facebook and Twitter. It allows you to focus on group audience which have the similar interests as of you and can be segregated based on geography, age, gender and preferences. This interactive platform helps you in multiple ways like promotion, increase trust value among the buyers as well to get the best feedback from the audience about your brand to improve it further for wonderful experience. At Opal Infotech, we explore the social media for maximum outreach to your prospective visitors.

There are several important and big Social Media Networks for Business Promotion:

Facebook:

Facebook is a user friendly social environment, where we can create your own business fan page to make brand impact and healthy conversation with your global audience by posting industry and product specific information, images, video etc. This way we can make your loyal and prospective client base in constant touch with your company brand can also increase the popularity in terms of Likes.

Twitter:

Twitter is a second most popular media marketing tool among the online community to share their views, opinions, promotions and get up-to-date information on regular basis by following the market leaders.

Google+:

It is Google's proprietary social network arising in completion with Facebook. Attention to this powerful media is also important because it has significant influence on Google search engine ranking process. You can upload the photos, videos, links and important information to share in your group members. We help you to promote your Google+ page with postings that will be liked and followed by visitors.

Pinterest:

Pinterest is a growing image centric platform used by all types of businesses as well for personal use, where you can upload the interesting images for visitors. One can even showcase products offerings and promoting the brand personality on pinboards.

LinkedIN

LinkedIn consist groups of professionals and best venue for interacting with the people of similar interest and industries. Getting good number of recommendations on LinkedIn is good for your business credibility which people rely more.
